About

Hi, I'm Mashiat Ferdous!
- a web developer with over 3 years of experience in designing and developing innovative digital solutions. I graduated from BRAC University with a degree in Computer Science and Engineering, and I currently work remotely as a web developer at Magic Office, a UK-based company.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the opportunity to work on a wide variety of projects—from large-scale applications to small business websites. I specialize in WordPress and have extensive knowledge of various CMS tools, ensuring every project I work on is both functional and visually engaging.

I’m passionate about creating user-friendly websites and web applications that provide great user experiences. I have a knack for taking complex ideas and turning them into easy-to-use designs. Whether it's enhancing functionality or crafting visually appealing designs, I always strive to ensure my work is easy to navigate and effective in solving client needs.
